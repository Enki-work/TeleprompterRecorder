Prompter Camera — the iPhone video recording app built specifically for YouTubers, VLoggers, and short-video creators.

Record your video while reading your pre-written script right on screen. The teleprompter overlay floats transparently over the live camera feed, keeping your eyes on the lens for a natural, confident look. Best of all, you can flip through script pages with the volume button, selfie timer button, or keyboard — completely hands-free, at your own pace.

KEY FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TELEPROMPTER & SCRIPT MANAGEMENT
• Script text floats transparently over the live camera preview
• Flip pages with the volume button (+/−) — no need to touch the screen
• Flip pages with the selfie timer button (Bluetooth remote shutter)
• Flip pages with the keyboard — full control at your own rhythm
• Adjustable frosted-glass blur (0–100%) keeps your footage clearly visible behind the text
• Gradient fade at the top for natural, professional-looking eye guidance
• Tap anywhere on the prompter to instantly show or hide it
• Script is automatically saved and restored every time you open the app

FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE APPEARANCE
• Choose any background color with the system color picker
• Choose any text color to match your style or lighting
• Adjust font size with a slider (12–60 pt)
• Fine-tune blur intensity in real time with live preview
• One-tap reset to restore default settings
• All customizations are saved automatically

MULTI-LENS & PROFESSIONAL VIDEO RECORDING
• Switch freely between all iPhone built-in lenses (wide, ultra-wide, telephoto)
• Up to 4K (3840×2160) resolution
• Also supports 2.7K, Full HD (1080p), HD (720p), and Standard Definition
• Pre-configured format presets — pick your preferred quality in one tap
• Choose from multiple frame rates: 24fps, 30fps, 60fps, and more
• HDR video: HEVC codec + HLG BT.2020 wide color for vivid, true-to-life footage
• SDR video: H.264 + Display P3 / sRGB for universal compatibility
• Recorded videos are automatically saved to your Photos library

CAMERA CONTROLS
• Switch between front and rear cameras with one tap
• Tap anywhere on the preview to set focus and exposure (touch focus)
• Background recording: recording continues safely even when you switch apps
